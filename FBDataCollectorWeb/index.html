About Facebook Data Collector

Why should you care?

What is it?

This Chrome Extension is designed to quantify the sources where data is collected from the user by Facebook. There are many sources that Facebook uses to get it's data, but this extension currently focuses on social plugins embedded on external websites and the time spent scrolling on Facebook.

  • Learn More

Why Does It Matter?

Facebook is increasingly the way we consume media and informational content. Facebook tailors the content and advertisements that it shows a user based on what it perceives that you want to see, and how much it is paid to sponsor content by external sources.

The content you see influences the way you think, your perspective on the world, and more importantly affects the content that you do not see. By keeping you into your own interests, Facebook is limiting your perspective on many topics and issues.
